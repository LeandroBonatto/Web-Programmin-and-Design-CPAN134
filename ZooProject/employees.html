Our Employee's

Here at Scarbourough zoo we have the best team of employee's who love and care for each and every one of our animals.

The main responsibilities of our employee's include:

  • keeping animal enclosures clean, germ-free and safely secured.
  • prepare food such as pellets, fresh produce, meat or hay.
  • observe animals and check for any signs of distress or ill health and administer medications.
  • design, build and repair environments which are as close to natural habitats as possible.
